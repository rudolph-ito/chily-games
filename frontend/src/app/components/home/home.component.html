Welcome! The following games are available to play:

Yaniv

A card game where you are trying to get the lowest amount of points.
Once you have less than 7 points, you can call Yaniv.
However if another player has a lower score you recieve a penalty.

Games

Oh Heck

A card game where you bet the number of tricks you'll take and get points if you match your bet.
For each trick, one player leads with a card and others must follow suit if possible, otherwise can play anything.
Highest card of the original suit wins unless any spades are present in which case the highest spade wins.
Match your bet to earn five points plus you bet. Otherwise you get 0 points for the round.
Play 7 or 14 rounds where the number of cards you start with each round changes.

Rummikub

A tile based game where you create sets. A set is a group of the same number with different colors or a run of sequential numbers of the same color. Your initial play must be at least 30 points and cannot modify existing sets. For all later plays, you may rearrange sets on the board in order to play tiles.
